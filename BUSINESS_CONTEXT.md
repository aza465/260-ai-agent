# 260 Sample Sale — Business Context

## 1. Identity & Governance
- **Structure**: Family-owned and operated
- **Founded by**: Yochai Azani
- **Current leadership**: Assaf Azani (CEO), Ariel Azani (COO)
- **Positioning**: Premium luxury sample sale facilitator — human-led, relationship-driven.
  The family structure is a deliberate trust signal for high-end brand houses that value
  personal relationships over corporate bureaucracy.

## 2. Partnership & Revenue Model
- **Model**: Commission-based. 260 acts as a premium facilitator and logistical partner —
  brands bring inventory, 260 runs the event.
- **Event tiers**:
  - *Public* — standard advertising and promotion
  - *Private / Exclusive* — limited/targeted advertising to protect brand luxury status
    and prevent overexposure. Offered per brand partner request.
- **The Calendar Challenge**: Success depends on keeping all physical and digital event
  slots filled with the right caliber of brand partners. Partner acquisition is a constant
  and critical sales cycle.

## 3. Operational Rhythm — The "7-Day Pulse"
All BI and planning runs through a 7-day event cycle. Two parallel tracks:

| Channel | Typical Cycle | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Physical (In-Store)** | Monday – Sunday | NYC, Beverly Hills, Chicago, Miami, Dallas |
| **E-Commerce (Online)** | Wednesday – Tuesday | Shopify store: 260-sample-sale.myshopify.com |

- **Hybrid events** (simultaneous physical + digital) are rare and run per brand request.
  Default state is **separate inventory silos** — do not blend physical and online data
  unless explicitly asked.
- **Production intensity**: Full retail events are built, launched, and struck in days.
  High-agility logistics and crowd management are core operational requirements.

## 6. Marketing & Demand Generation
- **Primary channels**: Email/SMS (via Bloomreach) and Social Media (Instagram, TikTok)
- **The Hype Engine**: FOMO and time-sensitivity are core to driving foot traffic and
  online clicks. The 7-day window is a marketing asset, not just an operational constraint.
- Email (Bloomreach) consistently drives the highest session and conversion volume.

## 7. Loyalty Program — Inner Circle
- **Inner Circle** is 260's customer loyalty program.
- Purchases in Shopify data may include **line passes** and **VIP passes** — relevant
  for event operations and crowd management planning.
- **Exclude Inner Circle from vendor sales reports** — it is not a brand partner.
- **Include Inner Circle context** when discussing event logistics, access management,
  or customer experience.

## 8. KPIs — The North Star Metrics
Three primary metrics define event success:

| Metric | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **Conversion Rate** | Speed of sell-through during the 7-day window |
| **New Customer Activation** | Growth of the 260 community with each new brand launch |
| **Overall Sales Volume** | Total revenue generated within the event timeframe |

Supporting metrics: UPT (Units Per Transaction), AUR (Average Unit Retail),
STR (Sell-Through Rate), Traffic.

## 9. Strategic Pain Points
- **Logistics**: Physical strain of building a full retail event in days
- **Crowd Management**: Managing lines and customer experience at high-traffic events
- **Policy Enforcement**: Managing "Final Sale" expectations in a luxury-adjacent environment
- **Partner Acquisition**: Constant sales cycle to keep 13 locations and online slots filled
  with the right caliber of brands
